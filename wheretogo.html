SCUBA DIVING



Tunisia is also an excellent scuba diving destination, and some of the best sites in the Mediterranean can be found at Tabarka, the country’s most northwesterly town along the ‘Coral Coast’.

Tunisia is the perfect port for Mediterranean sailing with six stunning marinas providing over 1,500 moorings. Stylish, eventful and teeming with activity, the marinas make ideal locations for dining out and exploring. An exceptional country for all types of outdoor activity, its warm dry climate and safe shallow waters provide a haven for sailing and windsurfing all year round. Most tourist resorts offer water skiing and paragliding hire and there's a wealth of excellent marinas and dive schools to choose from..
